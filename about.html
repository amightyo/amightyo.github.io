I was born in Calabar, Nigeria. I have an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Ilorin. I also have an MSc. in Computer Engineering from Istanbul Technical University. I am currently an MSc. student and a Graduate Research Assistant in Computer Science at Wayne State University. My interests lie in Robotics and Big Data. At this time, my research is based on leveraging High Performance Computing Clusters (HPCC) for Large-Scale Image Analysis to solve Big Data problems under the supervision of Professor Xuewen Chen.
